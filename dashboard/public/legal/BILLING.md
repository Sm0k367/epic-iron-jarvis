# Billing & Refunds Policy

**Epic Tech AI**  
**Effective date:** July 12, 2026  

Contact: [epictechai@gmail.com](mailto:epictechai@gmail.com)

## 1. Overview

Epic Tech AI is primarily a **local-first** product. Offline use with the mock model or local Ollama does **not** require payment.

Optional **commerce features** may include:

- Credit packs (microtransactions for metered cloud AI usage)
- Subscription plans (if offered)
- Marketplace or connector purchases (if offered)

Payments are processed by **Stripe**. We do not store full payment card numbers on our servers.

## 2. Credits

- Credits are a prepaid balance tracked in the local (or configured) ledger.
- Burning credits for model usage is estimated from token meters and may not match provider invoices exactly.
- Credits have no cash value except as required by mandatory law.
- Credits are non-transferable between unrelated users unless we state otherwise.

## 3. Prices and taxes

Prices are shown at checkout (dashboard, Telegram `/buy`, or API). Taxes may be added by Stripe based on location. We may change prices prospectively; changes do not retroactively alter completed purchases unless required by law.

## 4. Renewals (subscriptions)

If you subscribe to a recurring plan:

- Billing renews automatically until you cancel via the Stripe customer portal or in-product controls
- You authorize recurring charges for the plan you select
- Cancel before the renewal date to avoid the next period’s charge

## 5. Refunds

| Situation | Policy |
|-----------|--------|
| Duplicate / technical charge error | Contact us within 14 days; we will investigate and refund if verified |
| Unused credit pack purchased by mistake | Request within 14 days if **no credits were burned**; refund at our discretion |
| Partially used credits | Generally **non-refundable** |
| Dissatisfaction with third-party model quality | Directed to that provider; we do not refund third-party inference costs they billed you separately |
| Chargebacks | We may suspend commerce features pending resolution |

Email **epictechai@gmail.com** with subject `Billing` and your Stripe receipt / last4 / approximate time (never send full card numbers).

## 6. Chargebacks

If you dispute a charge with your bank, contact us first when possible. Fraudulent chargebacks may result in permanent loss of purchase privileges.

## 7. Free local use

Mock provider and local Ollama paths are intended to remain free of Epic Tech AI credit charges. Cloud providers you connect may still bill you under their own accounts.

## 8. Changes

We may update this policy by revising this document and the in-app Legal → Billing page.
